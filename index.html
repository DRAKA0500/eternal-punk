ABOUT NEONTHREADS

Born in the underground streetwear scene of Jakarta, NEONTHREADS merges cyberpunk aesthetics with contemporary street fashion.

We design for those who see the world differently - where the digital and physical realms blur into one.

Each piece is crafted with attention to detail, quality materials, and a rebellious spirit.
